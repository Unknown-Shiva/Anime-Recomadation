Every legend starts with a first episode.Begin your anime journey with these unforgettable series!

Anime for first timers

Death Note


Imagine finding a notebook that lets you kill anyone just by writing their name — scary, right? That’s what happens to Light Yagami, a genius student who decides to use it to “cleanse” the world of criminals. But his plan attracts the attention of L, a brilliant detective determined to stop him.


It’s a psychological thriller, mystery, and supernatural anime filled with mind games, tension, and moral questions. Every episode feels like a chess match between two geniuses. If you love clever plots, dark themes, and thrilling twists, Death Note is an absolute must-watch classic.

Hell’s Paradise


Gabimaru, a deadly ninja sentenced to death, is offered freedom if he finds the Elixir of Life on a mysterious island filled with monsters and killers. Alongside the executioner Sagiri, he faces twisted creatures and his own past.


It’s a dark fantasy, action, and psychological anime mixing brutal fights with emotional depth. The world is eerie yet beautiful, blending horror, philosophy, and redemption. The animation is top-tier, and the story questions what it means to live. If you liked Jujutsu Kaisen or Chainsaw Man, you’ll love this intense, beautifully crafted adventure.

Shikimori’s Not Just a Cutie


Meet Shikimori — she’s beautiful, kind, and has a cool, badass side that appears whenever her clumsy boyfriend Izumi gets into trouble. It’s a romantic comedy set in high school, but what makes it fun is how it flips the usual roles: the girl protects the boy, and she does it with style!


The show blends sweetness with comedy and little bursts of action-like flair. Their chemistry feels natural and adorable, with every episode showing small, heartwarming moments. If you like wholesome romance with a confident, charming heroine, this anime will make you smile.

Spy × Family


Meet Loid Forger, a top spy on a secret mission who must build a fake family to blend in — but things get hilarious when his “wife” Yor turns out to be an assassin and their “daughter” Anya can read minds!


It’s a comedy, action, and slice-of-life anime with a perfect mix of heartwarming moments and undercover chaos. Each episode balances humor, family bonds, and thrilling missions. The dynamic between the Forgers is adorable and full of surprises. Watch it for laughs, love, and an unexpectedly wholesome spy adventure.

The Apothecary Diaries


Imagine stepping into an ancient palace full of secrets, perfumes, and poisons. That’s where Maomao, a clever young apothecary, finds herself after being forced to work as a servant. She’s curious, sharp-tongued, and can’t resist solving mysteries — especially when they involve strange illnesses or suspicious deaths.


It’s a historical mystery drama that feels calm yet exciting, like reading a detective story wrapped in royal silk. The art is gorgeous, the dialogue witty, and Maomao’s logic is so satisfying to watch. If you enjoy clever problem-solving and elegant palace intrigue, this anime is a hidden gem.

Blue Lock


What if soccer was treated like a survival game? Blue Lock throws 300 of Japan’s best young strikers into an intense training program to create the ultimate ego-driven forward. Among them is Isagi Yoichi, who must outsmart and outscore everyone to secure his dream.


It’s a sports, psychological, and action anime that feels like Squid Game meets Haikyuu!!. The matches are wild, emotional, and full of strategy. Characters have huge personalities, rivalries burn hot, and the animation is fire. Watch it if you love competitive spirit, insane twists, and that “win at any cost” energy.
